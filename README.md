Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard
📌 Overview

Developed an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze Superstore sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and revenue trends.

The project focuses on identifying high-performing categories, customer contribution, discount impact, and forecasting future revenue and profit trends.

🛠 Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Pivot Charts
Forecasting
Data Cleaning
Conditional Formatting
KPI Analysis
📊 Key Metrics
Total Revenue
Total Profit
Profit Margin
Average Discount
Revenue Growth (YoY)
Profit Growth (YoY)
📈 Key Insights
Technology category generated the highest profit.
High discount percentages reduced profitability significantly.
A small percentage of customers contributed a major share of total revenue.
Revenue showed strong year-over-year growth trends.
